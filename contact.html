Graduate Students

The Bioelectronics Group is looking for motivated researchers at the interface of materials science, neuroscience, and bioengineering. Graduate students admitted to MIT in the departments of Materials Science and Engineering, Brain and Cognitive Sciences, Biology, or the Harvard-MIT Health Sciences and Technology program are encouraged to contact Prof. Anikeeva by email. (Please note that all students must apply to those programs directly for admittance to MIT first before joining any research lab.)

Post-Doctoral Researchers

The Bioelectronics Group welcomes applications from talented postdoctoral candidates with expertise in neuroscience, magnetic nanomaterials, fiber fabrication, optogenetics, or related fields. Interested applicants should send the following to anikeeva@mit.edu:

  • Curriculum Vitae (including complete list of publications)
  • Brief (1–2 page) statement of past research experience and research interests in the Bioelectronics Group
Applicants should also be prepared to provide 3 letters of recommendation upon request.
